Since 1978

Our History

Most of the original players came up through rugby at UCSB and weren't ready to quit when they aged out of the college game. The side first played together in 1978, formally organized as a club in 1980, and has been a Santa Barbara institution ever since.

The Shirt

Santa Barbara, worn on the chest

Blue for the ocean, green for the mountains, split by a gray stripe for Highway 101.

1978

First side takes the field

UCSB graduates working in restaurants around town started playing together; Lance Mason would serve as first president. The name was deliberately tongue-in-cheek: the grunion, the small fish famous for its moonlit runs on local beaches. As Mason put it, there was always the question of whether they even existed.

1980

Officially founded

The club is formally established for the promotion, encouragement, and extension of Rugby Union football.

1981

An instant family

Members came from every sport and walk of life looking for community — and found it.

1998

The Twenty Greatest Grunion

To mark the 20th anniversary, the club named its Twenty Greatest — the start of a formal Hall of Fame.

2006

Champions

A divisional title — a high-water mark the club would chase for years.

2018

Forty years on

Charter members traveled from as far as Austin, Texas, to watch the current side play the Fossils at Elings Park.

2022

SoCal Division 3 champions

A late try and conversion sealed a 27–27 draw with San Luis Obispo, clinching the title on points differential — the first championship since 2006 — and a regional qualifier for USA Rugby Nationals.

2023

Bringing back the 7s

After a five-year hiatus, the club revived the Santa Barbara 7s at Elings Park.

Today

A new era

Investing in players, coaching, and club infrastructure — and still playing the rough-and-tumble rugby the club was built on.

The '78 Club

Named for the year it all started, The '78 Club is the Grunion's circle of Legacy Donors — funding the club's next era. Gifts are tax-deductible (SBRFC is a 501(c)(3)) and go to player acquisition, coaching, and club infrastructure.

Santa Barbara's Rugby Family

The Grunion is the men's side of a wider Santa Barbara rugby community. If you're looking for women's or youth rugby, our sister clubs would love to hear from you.

Women's Rugby

Santa Barbara Mermaids

Competitive women's rugby in Santa Barbara since 2015, playing in the Southern California Senior Women's Division II. Open to all women and nonconforming players 18+.

Visit sbwomensrugby.com →
Youth Rugby

Santa Barbara Stingrays

Youth rugby from U8 to U18 (U8 plays non-contact flag), building skill, character, and teamwork. Newcomers always welcome; scholarships and financial aid available.
